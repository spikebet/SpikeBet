Spike Play – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

 

General Questions

 

What is Spike Play?

Spike Play is a sweepstakes-based platform where users make predictions on Track & Field events. It is designed for entertainment purposes only and does not involve gambling, betting, or wagering.

 

Is Spike Play legal?

Yes. Spike Play operates under a sweepstakes model that complies with applicable laws. No purchase is necessary to participate, and making a purchase does not increase your chances of winning.

 

How do I participate?

To participate, create an account and use either Spike Coins (for social play) or Spike Cash (for sweepstakes entries). Predictions are placed using these virtual currencies, and winnings are distributed according to our rules.

 

Spike Coins & Spike Cash

 

What is the difference between Spike Coins and Spike Cash?

●       Spike Coins: A virtual currency used for free social play. They have no real-world value and cannot be redeemed for prizes.

●       Spike Cash: A sweepstakes currency used to enter contests for a chance to win redeemable prizes.

 

How do I earn Spike Cash?

Spike Cash can be obtained in several ways:

1.       Daily login rewards

2.       Promotional giveaways

3.       Bonus rewards with certain purchases of Spike Coins

4.       Mail-in requests (as outlined in our official rules)

 

Can I buy Spike Cash?

No. Spike Cash is only available through free methods, such as giveaways or as a bonus with Spike Coin purchases.

 

How does redemption work?

Only Used Spike Cash—earned through winning in sweepstakes contests—can be redeemed for real-world prizes. A minimum of 20 Used Spike Cash is required for withdrawal.

 

Making Predictions & Winnings

 

How do I make a prediction?

Select an event, choose an outcome, and enter with either Spike Coins or Spike Cash. Entries go into a shared pool, and winnings are distributed among those with correct predictions.

 

How are payouts determined?

Payouts are calculated based on the total pool of entries and the number of correct predictions. The minimum payout is always equal to the amount of Spike Cash you used to enter, provided you are the only winner.

 

Are there different payout structures for "To Win," "Top 2," and "Top 3" picks?

Yes, but we do not display odds, as payout amounts fluctuate until the event concludes. Final winnings are determined based on total entries and correct picks.

 

What happens if an athlete does not start or does not finish a race?

●       Did Not Start (DNS): Your entry is refunded.

●       Did Not Finish (DNF): The athlete is considered to have finished last. "Over" time picks win, while "Under" and placement picks lose.

 

Can I get a refund on my entries or purchases?

No. All purchases and contest entries are final.

 

Account & Support

 

How do I withdraw my winnings?

You can request a withdrawal at any time via your profile or the in-app shop, provided you have at least 20 Used Spike Cash. Withdrawals are processed in USD through an authorized payment provider.

 

Where is Spike Play available?

Spike Play is available in the United States, except in the following states: Connecticut, Delaware, Idaho, Michigan, Mississippi, Montana, Nevada, Washington, and West Virginia.

 

How can I provide feedback or ask further questions?

We welcome feedback! Use the feedback form linked in the app's sidebar or reach out to us at contact@spikeplay.com.

 

Quick Reference

 

Minimum payout if won?

Always your exact Spike Coins / Cash at least. This will only happen if you're the only person to place a winning pick on a given event.

 

Are odds different for coins vs. cash?

Nope

 

How do I know odds for to win, top 2, or top 3?

We don't show odds for any place picks because they change until the race starts. We calculate payouts for to win, top 2, and top 3 after each event concludes.

 

How does making picks even work?

When you make a pick, your Spike Coins / Cash are placed into a pool of all other Spike Coins / Cash. We then distribute lost Coins among people who pick correctly!

 

How can I redeem winnings?

Withdraw at any time from your profile or from our in-app shop. You need at least 20.00 redeemable ("Used") Spike Cash to be able to make a withdrawal. You obtain redeemable Spike Cash by making correct picks!

 

What happens if DNS / DNF?

●       DNS: Since the athlete has not competed at all, we return your exact amount of Spike Coins/Cash.

●       DNF: Since the athlete entered the race but did not finish, we count the athlete as finishing in last place. Any "over" picks on time are counted as wins, any "under" picks on time are counted as losses, and any place picks are counted as losses. We'll never offer picks on any pacers.

 

Are there any refunds?

All purchases are final.

 

Can I give you feedback?

Yes! We love feedback. Please let us know your thoughts/suggestions in the form linked in the sidebar on the top left of all screens.
